Privacy Policy for WordWar

Effective Date: July 14, 2024

Welcome to WordWar! This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal data when you use our app, WordWar, which is designed for games involving words, such as word puzzles.

WordWar does not collect any personal data from its users. We respect your privacy and ensure that your personal information remains secure.

Data Storage

The app stores data locally on your device. This data includes your user preferences and game progress. This information is stored solely on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

WordWar does not use any third-party services that might collect or process your data.

Privacy Policy Updates
