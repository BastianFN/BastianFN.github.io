Privacy Policy for RKG

Last updated: April 8, 2024

Thank you for choosing to use RKG ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users of our app. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use RKG and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our app, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, and postal address. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our app

3. Data Transfer

Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from your jurisdiction.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

4. Data Disclosure

We may disclose your personal data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of RKG
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the app
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the app or the public
  • Protect against legal liability

5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
